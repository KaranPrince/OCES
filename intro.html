Online Case Enrollment System

The Online Case Enrollment System is designed to modernize and simplify the process of registering and managing cases. It eliminates inefficiencies such as excessive paperwork and delays, providing a seamless, transparent, and user-friendly digital platform. Users can easily file cases, while police and administrators can manage and resolve them effectively. By promoting accountability and offering real-time updates, the system bridges the gap between citizens and authorities, ensuring faster and more reliable case management.
